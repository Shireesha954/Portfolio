Fig. 01 — Bio

About

I trained as an industrial designer before moving into software, and it still shows: I care about the weight of a button, the click of a toggle, the exact millisecond a transition should take. For the past seven years I've split my time between product engineering and independent studio work.

Most recently I led frontend architecture for a transit agency's rider-facing apps, where accessibility wasn't a checklist item but a daily constraint that made the product better for everyone. Outside client work, I build small machines: light installations, sound toys, and looms that draw with thread instead of ink.

I'm currently based in Hyderabad, working with teams across time zones, and taking on a small number of new collaborations each quarter.
