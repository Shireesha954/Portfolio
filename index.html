I'm Maya Chen, a creative technologist and frontend engineer. I design and build accessible web tools, generative-art systems, and the occasional physical instrument that talks to a browser.

What I do

Build

Design systems, component libraries, and production frontends for teams who care about performance and detail. Every interface ships with keyboard support and screen-reader testing baked in, not bolted on.

Make

Generative art, sound toys, and small hardware experiments that live at the edge of code and craft. Several have shown at local maker fairs and gallery pop-ups.
